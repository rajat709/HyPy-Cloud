FAQs

Can we try HyPy cloud for free ?
Absolutely! At HyPy Cloud, we offer a 7-day free trial, allowing you to explore and experience our services before committing to a service plan. Take advantage of this trial period to test our platform and ensure it meets your requirements.
Is there any Runtime Limit for JupyterLAB?
Indeed, There is Not any Runtime Limit for Jupyter notebooks on our platform. However, we encourage users to utilize resources wisely and be mindful of their usage to ensure fair access for all users. Enjoy the flexibility and freedom to work on your Jupyter notebooks without any imposed time restrictions.
Can I customize my JupyterLAB ?
Yes! Experience the convenience of a personalized Jupyter notebook according to your needs, complete with a customized sub-domain URL. Benefit from pre-installed libraries and unleash the power of AI LAB with GPT support for enhanced productivity. Additionally, you have the flexibility to upgrade your backend requirements and storage space as needed.
Can I Access QPU and Q-Simulators ?
Yes! At hyPy Cloud, you can access QPUs and Quantum Simulators for your quantum computing needs. Enjoy up to 7 physical qubits for Free with the option to upgrade to more powerful QPUs as per your requirements. The flexibility scales with your payment, allowing you to leverage advanced quantum computing resources for your Quantum Projects.
How to deploy Apps in HyPy cloud ?
Once you have an app hosting plan, you will receive an email containing your credentials and a deployment portal link. Simply upload your app files through the portal, and within minutes, your web app will be live on the internet. We will send you a customized sub-domain app link via email. Please ensure that your app files are correct and valid before deploying. In case of any issues, you can easily re-upload your files.
How to host Anvil Apps in HyPy Cloud ?
To host your Anvil app, please provide an Anvil Uplink key and server code. Note that in the server code, do not include the command run_forever(), as our server is already running continuously. Once you provide the necessary files, your Anvil app will be live and ready to use or you can directly host it from our deployment portal.
Can We Upgrade the Machine & Storage ?
Yes! At HyPy Cloud, we offer the flexibility to upgrade your machine and storage according to your specific needs. We provide options with GPU support as well. Simply reach out to us and define your requirements, and our team will assist you in finding the ideal solution. Contact Us
How do I get my personalized subdomain URL ?
During the deployment process of your web app or JupyterLab. You have the option to enter a personalized Sub-domain URL. If left blank, a random URL will be automatically assigned as the default.
